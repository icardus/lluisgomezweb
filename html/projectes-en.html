PROJECTS

I am available to perform live in various different formats according to your needs, from a solo musician to a quartet or more. My repertoire and skill set cover a wide musical range, incorporating Folk, Bluegrass, Blues, Swing, and Jazz. As such, I can easily adapt to whatever type of concert you require, whether that be instructional workshops, festivals, or concerts.

Depending on what the situation requires, I can play acoustically without amplification or bring my own sound system.

Soloist

As a soloist, I have a large repertoire on the banjo, mandolin, and violin. Depending on the needs of the situation, I can perform vocal numbers, instrumentals, or a mixture of both.

Gypsy Jazz

Manouche, or Gypsy Jazz, is a style of acoustic music created by the legendary guitarist Django Reindhardt. I am often called to perform this type of music in various ensembles. It is a type of jazz that is fresh and easy to listen to, and I would be more than happy to arrange an ensemble for you. Trio and Quartet ensemble with guitarist Valentí Moya.

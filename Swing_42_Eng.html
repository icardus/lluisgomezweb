A journey through the music of Django Reinhardt and Swing.


A new project from four musicians with a wide trajectory on the scene, based on Manouche, a style created by Django Reinhardt and reinterpreted with the 5-String banjo and the mandolin, accompanied with the solid rhythmic base of the Manouche guitar and double-bass.

Swing 42 takes it’s name from one of the Django's most popular tunes to offer something different from traditional Swing, confident and happy, an acoustic music concert full of emotions.

Valentín Moya- Manouche Guitar / Lluís Gómez- 5-Sring Banjo

Oriol Gonzàlez- Mandolin / Maribel Rivero- Upright Bass

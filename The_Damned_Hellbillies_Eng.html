The bluegrass music is a recreational tool, it is music that has served for years to the Mountaineers of eastern United States to enjoy the moments in family and have fun! Exactly .. that's what these four musicians has led them to create this band. Keep the spirit of family meetings and celebrations. Every Sunday they meet at bluegrass jam sessions and old time music in Barcelona, Richard da Silva (mandolin and vocals) and Lluis Gomez (banjo, fiddle and vocals) are the promotors of these meetings where the latter coincidide with Maribel Rivero (double bass and vocals) within the prestigious Barcelona Bluegrass Band. The quartet is completed by the presence of Xavi Ollé (guitar, banjo and vocals) great fan of country music and American roots. We leave to the front porch to sit in a bail of hay and enjoy a journey through the music of the Appalachian Mountains because we're getting hitched!




Richard Da Silva- Mandolin & Vocals / Xavi Ollé- Guitar & Vocals

Lluís Gómez- 5-String Banjo, Fiddle & Vocals / Maribel Rivero- Upright Bass & Vocals
